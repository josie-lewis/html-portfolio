The Best Movies of All Time - According to Josie

My Top 3 Films

1. About Time

Beautifully poignant film about coming to terms with the passing of time, and acceptance of life's twists and turns.

2. The Secret Life of Walter Mitty

A very cinematically beautiful film, creatively produced. It takes you on an adventure and inspires you to step out. 10/10 love.

3. The Polar Express

Yes, I know, it is a christmas film.
But it still deserves a place in my top 3 films.
A beautifully crafed film about belief, courage, and adventure.
